Traditional Cards Against Humanity game moved to online experience! App supports a kid friendly version, hence Cards For Humanity

Powered by Vue.js in the front and communicated through Socket.io

Supports full and complete sequence diagram from creating a game room to selecting victorious cards.

Objectives
  1. Each player takes turn to be the Card Czar and plays a Black Card
  2. Everyone else answers the question by choosing one White card that fills in the blank
  3. The Card Czar shuffles all of the answers and shares each card combination and then picks a favorite
  4. Whoever played that answer keeps the Black Card as one Awesome Point
  5. Player with most points wins.
  6. It is recommended to use a teleconference software like Zoom to chat.
